Our Story

IQIC ( The International Quality Inspection Company ) is an independent company covering quality inspection services in the ceramic industry, located in the world's second-largest ceramic hub, Morbi, India.

We are one of the leading QUALITY INSPECTION COMPANIES based in Morbi, India, serving for more than 5 years.

The International Quality Inspection Company specializes in quality assurance & inspection for ceramic wall tiles, ceramic floor tiles, porcelain (vitrified) tiles, sanitary wares, marble, granite, and quartz stone.

Built on Competence, Certainty, Quality, Neutrality, and Objectivity, IQIC closely collaborates with customers, providing need-based customized services to meet demanding market requirements.

We are unique as our entire team is qualified ceramic engineers with over 5+ years of experience in the ceramic industry. We ensure our services are aligned with ISO & European standards.

Additional Information

We ensure all testing of tiles is conducted according to ISO & European standards. Our dedicated team is here to provide exceptional quality assurance.
