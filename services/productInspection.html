1

Pre-Production Inspection

Before production starts, raw materials and equipment are inspected to ensure they meet quality standards, preventing issues during the manufacturing process.

2

Initial Production Inspection

At the beginning of production, the first batch of tiles is checked to ensure it aligns with required specifications, allowing for early detection of potential defects.

3

During Production Inspection

While production is ongoing, random checks are performed to maintain consistent quality throughout the manufacturing process.

4

Pre-Shipment Inspection

Before shipment, the final tiles undergo a thorough inspection to ensure they meet size, color, and quality requirements, guaranteeing customer satisfaction.

5

Container Loading Supervision

During loading, we supervise the packing process to ensure tiles are securely packed and the shipment matches the client's order, preventing damage during transit.
