Types Of Inspaction

01. Pre Production Inspection

It's important to verify whether raw material quality is ok or not, it will also help to verify the production with all your interests.

Key Checks:
  • ✓ Samples matched with master tile
  • ✓ Production preparation verification
  • ✓ Buyer's requirements understanding
  • ✓ Delay assessment
Benefits:

Solve quality problems which may occur during manufacturing process.

02. Initial Production Inspection

The main objective is to ensure the production process meets Buyer's specifications from the beginning.

Key Checks:
  • ✓ Vendor requirements compliance
  • ✓ Quality verification from start
  • ✓ Packaging & Labelling inspection
  • ✓ Barcode Verification
Benefits:
  • ✓ Confirm production process effectiveness
  • ✓ Early detection of non-conformities
  • ✓ Preventive problem solving

03. During Production Inspection

Focuses on timely corrective actions and quality improvements during the production process.

Key Checks:
  • ✓ Detailed production process verification
  • ✓ Quality parameters maintenance
  • ✓ Production process control
  • ✓ Master sample comparison
Benefits:

Prevents expansion of faults moving in full production

04. Pre Shipment Inspection

Final quality check before products leave the factory, ensuring compliance with specifications.

Key Benefits:
  • ✓ Requirements compliance verification
  • ✓ Reputation protection
  • ✓ Specifications adherence
  • ✓ Source quality verification
  • ✓ Shipping marks verification

05. Container Loading Supervision

Final inspection during container loading to ensure proper shipping conditions.

Benefits:
  • ✓ Container condition verification
  • ✓ Loading guidelines compliance
  • ✓ Quantity and quality assurance
  • ✓ Wrong product loading prevention
  • ✓ Packaging information verification
  • ✓ Export cartons condition check
