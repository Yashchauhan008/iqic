Why Us

Why Us

AQS (Asian Quality Service) is an independent company covering quality inspection services in the ceramic industry, located in the world's second-largest ceramic hub, Morbi, India.

We are one of the leading QUALITY INSPECTION COMPANIES based in Morbi, India, serving for more than 10 years.

Asian Quality Services specializes in quality assurance & inspection for ceramic wall tiles, ceramic floor tiles, porcelain (vitrified) tiles, sanitary wares, marble, granite, and quartz stone.

Built on Competence, Certainty, Quality, Neutrality, and Objectivity, AQS closely collaborates with customers, providing need-based customized services to meet demanding market requirements.

We are unique as our entire team is qualified ceramic engineers with over 10+ years of experience in the ceramic industry. We ensure our services are aligned with ISO & European standards.
